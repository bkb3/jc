Introduction

  • The demonstration shows a simplied version of phase separation.
  • Phase separation is responsible for membraneless compartments.
  • Disordered proteins are abundant in membraneless compartments.

Methods

  • LAF-1 protein from Caenorhabditis elegans was used as a model protein.
  • LAF-1 is found in P granules and phase spearates in vitro.
  • Disordered region has an abundance of RGG motif.

Conclusions and my thoughts

  • Small conserved motif was found to be important in phase separation.
  • Charge separation increases the critical temperature (Tc) and phase separation propensity.
  • Deletion of Arginine and Tyrosine decreases Tc.
    • Very interesting and potentially generalisable.
  • Although the WT is disordered, other variants may have some differences in disorder. This was not calculated (somewhat related term Rg was calculated).
